Earlier this year we worked with Conflux to help map out how we could best deliver value to our customers.

We'd already done plenty of mapping using Team Topologies methods internally, but we kept hitting the same problem: how do we satisfy three different customer segments, across a series of shared platforms, without massively increasing our cost base?

Working with Matthew Skelton, Rich Allen and the team at Conflux helped us unlock this. We brought product, engineering and domain experts together, mapped out user needs, identified blockers to flow, found bounded contexts and mapped independent service heuristics.

While this did ultimately lead to a restructuring of our teams, the real value was the set of tools we are now using to gradually evolve our organisation as we grow and our constraints change.

We now have a nimble organisation and the techniques needed to adapt to change, identify trade-offs, and measure our effectiveness.
