Google's CEO says AI now writes about 25% of Google's code. Meta's CEO claims that within 18 months all code will be written by AI. Anthropic's CEO thinks we'll have that in 12. Microsoft's CEO says 20–30% of its codebase is already AI-written. Salesforce's CEO puts its AI-built workflows at 30–50%.

I use AI every day and it does make me more productive. But more AI-generated code or content isn't always better.

All these executives sell AI products. Their stats fuel hype, drive sales and lift share prices. Don't be swayed by slick soundbites.

Generative AI shines when you sense-check its output. It excels at some tasks and struggles with others.

Cursor has reignited my passion for side projects. Claude Code offers a genuine glimpse of the future. ChatGPT shapes my professional and personal routines. Lovable is now a favourite game with my kids, who can vibe-code too.

AI-assisted creation is the future. Don't let snake-oil pitches blind you.
